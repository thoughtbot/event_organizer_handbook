# Event Organizer Handbook

A practical guide to starting and running a tech meetup. Whether there is no meetup
in your city, yours stopped running, or you have been attending someone else's for years
and think you could do it yourself, this handbook walks you through every step: picking a
name, finding speakers, sponsors, and a venue, running the event itself, and what to do
the day after.

The knowledge comes from running [RubyMX](https://comunidadruby.mx/en), a Ruby meetup
in Guadalajara, Mexico that started with five people in a coworking space and has been
running monthly ever since.
